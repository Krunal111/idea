About Us

IDEA is An organization founded by a group of intellectual enthusiastics and works basically in the field of Disaster & Emergency Management.

To make INDIA RESILIENT and a safe and a smarter place to dwell. Making people more self sufficient and incorporating skill based knowledge into day to day activities is our prime goal.

With this vision, we bring to you innovative solutions in diverse fields ranging from research, mental health, hospital safety, education to risk assessment and multi-hazard mapping. We collaborate with various Community Based Organizations (CBOs) and also assist the government at all levels, working to serve the same purpose. With a motto to serve humanity and channelizing the positivity of life into something innovative and helpful with us, we can assure you can “Indulge with Desire, Emerge through Action”.

What We Do

IDEA's prime focus is to strengthen community resilience to reduce the negative impacts of disasters. We assess what the community needs, what the community has as a prerequisite to train the communities to develop their resilience and empower them to face the disaster. We constantly re-innovate and try to move away from traditional practices and institutionalize futuristic methods of operation for augmenting resilience in healthcare. We are constantly reinventing the way training is imparted by  introducing Simulation, Real time goal based Capacity building and interactive learning.
